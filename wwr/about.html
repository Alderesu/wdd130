Welcome to White Water Rafting!

  We offer our clients a outstanding and daring outdoor experience on White Water Rapids. Our course have been thoughtfully planned out by our experts and have been tested throughout the time, and we provide guided rafting adventures on rivers that ranges in difficulties from serene floats to heart-pounding rapids that will awake the adventure within you!

History

 Founded in 1824, "White Water Rafting" has been a pioneer in the White Water rafting industry for two centuries. The company was started by a group of adventurous explorers who were captivated by the thrill of navigating the wild rapids of the Colorado River. Over the years, White Water Rafting has built a reputation for providing safe and exhilarating rafting experiences, while also educating customers about the importance of environmental stewardship and the preservation of natural resources. Today, White Water Rafting is still family-owned and operated, and continues to be a leader in the White Water rafting industry, offering a wide range of trips and adventures for thrill-seekers of all ages and skill levels.
